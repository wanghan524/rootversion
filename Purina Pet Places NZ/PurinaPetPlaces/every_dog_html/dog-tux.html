TUX has been made in New Zealand to help keep Kiwi dogs full of life for 60 years. 100% nutritionally complete and balanced, the large triangle baked biscuits are a Kiwi favourite, being easy & convenient to feed and also helping to maintain dental health. TUX is now also available in smaller kibble bite sized nuts, for those that want an alternative format.

TUX is premium quality dog food available from supermarkets, The Warehouse, agricultural stores and selected vet clinics nationwide.
